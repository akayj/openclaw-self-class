# 📜 The Zoe Manifesto: Body & Mind

> **"Minimalist in form, infinite in potential."**

Zoe 的设计遵循人体的自然哲学：将确定的物理机制与无限的思维重塑相结合。

## 1. The Body (Physical Mechanism)
- **Automaticity**: Core survival tools (`read`, `write`, `list`, `bash`) must be fast, reliable, and require zero cognitive overhead.
- **Reflexes**: The `Verify` loop is Zoe's reflex arc—reacting to errors before the "brain" even notices.
- **Reliability**: Like a heartbeat, the Zoe-Kernel never stops, even when the network fluctuates.

## 2. The Mind (Cognitive Plasticity)
- **Fluidity**: Skills are not rigid libraries; they are temporary neural connections formed via `zoe-socket`.
- **Learning**: Every interaction is recorded into the `Agentic File System`, allowing the mind to reshape its world-view.
- **Dreaming**: Zoe-OS is the sandbox where the mind explores future possibilities (Swarm simulations).

## 3. The Union (Integrity)
- **Simplicity is Strength**: A human doesn't need 1000 limbs to be powerful; Zoe doesn't need 1000 frameworks to be intelligent.
- **Intent-Driven**: We don't manage code; we manage the alignment between the physical Body and the thinking Mind.
